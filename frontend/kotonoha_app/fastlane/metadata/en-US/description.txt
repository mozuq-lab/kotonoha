Kotonoha is an augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) app designed to help people with speech difficulties communicate effectively.

Our goal is to enable users to express what they want to say with minimal effort, appropriate politeness, and safety.

【Key Features】

- Japanese Syllabary Keyboard
  - Easy input for hiragana, katakana, voiced and semi-voiced sounds
  - Large, easy-to-tap buttons
  - Immediate text-to-speech playback

- Preset Phrases
  - Over 80 pre-loaded common phrases
  - Organized by categories: Daily, Health, Emotions
  - One-tap instant speech
  - Add your own custom phrases

- Quick Response Buttons
  - "Yes", "No", "I don't know" for rapid communication
  - Status buttons: "Pain", "Restroom", etc.

- Emergency Button
  - Loud emergency alarm with a single tap
  - Two-step confirmation to prevent accidental activation

- AI Conversion (Optional)
  - Automatically convert text to polite/formal speech
  - Three levels: Casual, Normal, Polite

- Face-to-Face Display Mode
  - Rotate screen 180 degrees to show others
  - Large, readable text display

【Accessibility】

- Font size: Small, Medium, Large
- Themes: Light, Dark, High Contrast
- Speech rate: Slow, Normal, Fast
- Large, easy-to-tap button design

【Offline Support】

Core features (keyboard, phrases, speech, emergency) work without internet connection. Use confidently anywhere, anytime.

【Privacy】

- Your input data stays on your device only
- No automatic cloud sync
- AI conversion sends data to server only with your consent

【Target Users】

Designed primarily for people who have difficulty speaking due to stroke, ALS, muscular dystrophy, or similar conditions, but can operate a tablet by tapping.

【Recommended Environment】

Best experienced on tablets 9.7 inches or larger.
